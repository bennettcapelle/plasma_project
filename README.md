Comparing Different Regions Across Several Fusion Devices

Fall ECE 525 Plasma Exploration Project

How to use:
Add or remove devices from the parameters section. Copy format of other devices. Create new plots to compare and contrast devices.

Primary Reference: P. Friedberg, Plasma Physics and Fusion Energy (Cambridge, 2007)



Plots Available to Generate:


Temp_vs_Density- Visual paramter space and compare to lines of constant Debye Length.

DebyeRatio_vs_Radius- Main criterion established by Friedberg- must be less than one.

PlasmaParameter_vs_Radius- Comparison of the argument of Coulomb's logarithm. Also marked on the y-axis is the relation of plasma parameter to collision frequency.

collision_frequency- Raw collision frequency plotted against size.
